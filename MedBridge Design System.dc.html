09 — Voice

How MedBridge talks

Do
  • Plain, short sentences. One idea per line.
  • Sentence case everywhere, including headings.
  • Verbs in buttons: “Register patient”, “Sign & share”.
  • Reassure on risky steps: “The report is signed and sent to ABDM.”
Don’t
  • No jargon or system codes in user-facing copy.
  • No Title Case or ALL-CAPS shouting.
  • No vague buttons like “Submit” or “OK”.
  • No cute or playful tone — this is a medical record.
